About AB KICKS LLC

Who We Are

AB KICKS LLC is a small, independent reseller based in North Haven, Connecticut, specializing in factory-sealed LEGO® sets. We're passionate collectors and enthusiasts who understand the value and joy that LEGO® brings to builders of all ages.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide collectors and LEGO® fans with access to retired, exclusive, and hard-to-find LEGO® sets that are no longer available through traditional retail channels. We carefully source and store our inventory to ensure that every set we sell is in pristine, factory-sealed condition.

What Makes Us Different

As a small business and reseller, we pride ourselves on:

  • Authenticity: Every set we sell is 100% genuine and factory-sealed
  • Quality: We inspect each set and ensure proper storage conditions
  • Transparency: Clear pricing, condition reporting, and SKU tracking
  • Customer Care: Personal attention to each order and inquiry
  • Secure Packaging: Professional packing to protect box condition during shipping

For Collectors & Builders

Whether you're a serious collector looking for retired sets, an adult fan of LEGO® (AFOL) seeking display pieces, or someone searching for that perfect gift, we're here to help. Our catalog features sets from various themes including Star Wars, Disney, Minecraft, LEGO Ideas, and more.

Contact Us

We're always happy to answer questions about our inventory, discuss specific sets, or help you find that elusive piece for your collection. Feel free to reach out to us anytime.
