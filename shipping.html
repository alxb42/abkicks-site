Shipping Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days (excluding weekends and holidays). During high-volume periods, processing may take slightly longer.

Shipping Methods

We ship from the United States using reputable carriers (e.g., USPS/UPS/FedEx depending on destination and package size). Carrier selection may vary based on cost, speed, and package requirements.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs (if applicable) are displayed at checkout or communicated prior to purchase when an order is placed through an invoice.

Tracking

When available, tracking information is provided after shipment. Delivery timelines are estimates and may vary due to carrier delays or weather.

Packaging

Sealed items are shipped using protective packaging intended to reduce transit damage. Please retain all packaging materials until your order is inspected.

Incorrect Address / Delivery Issues

  • Please ensure your shipping address is accurate before completing your purchase.
  • If a package is returned to sender due to an incorrect address, reshipment may require additional postage.
  • If tracking shows “delivered” but you cannot locate the package, contact the carrier first and then email us for assistance.

Questions? Contact Support or email support@abkicksllc.com.
